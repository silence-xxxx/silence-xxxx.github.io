<blockquote>
<p><em>Saga · The Circle</em> is a deeply personal work shaped by a clear creative vision.<br>
The game is built around interpretation, atmosphere, and the quiet space between words. Like the legends that inspired it, not everything is meant to be fully explained. Some meanings are left for each player to discover and carry in their own way.</p>

<p>I sincerely welcome the different emotions, interpretations, and discussions players may bring to this world. At the same time, I hope the same respect given to the creative boundaries of novels, music, and film can exist here as well.</p>

<p>This is not a refusal of dialogue, but a respect for perspective:<br>

<h2>About the Game</h2>

<h3>Build the Round Table. Watch It Break.</h3>

<p><strong class="highlight">Saga · The Circle</strong> is a literary CRPG inspired by the imagination and tension of tabletop roleplaying, focused on reading, interpretation, and meaningful choices.</p>

<p>Where Arthurian legend meets political tragedy, you watch King Arthur rise, gather the Knights of the Round Table yourself, and watch them tear the kingdom apart.</p>

<p>Travel across a fractured Britain haunted by monsters, invaders, prophecies, and disorder. Investigate murders in the eastern territories. Hunt witches in cursed swamps. Embark on the quest for the Holy Grail beyond fog-covered deserts and forgotten ruins.</p>

<p>But as the kingdom grows, so do its cracks.</p>

<p>Some knights believe Arthur’s dream can save Britain. Others believe it will destroy it. And beside the Round Table, Mordred is waiting for your answer.</p>

<hr>

<h2>Your Choices Shape the Knight You Become</h2>

<p>Your journey is not driven by simple good-and-evil systems.</p>

<ul>
<li>Idealism or Realism</li>
<li>Loyalty or Independence</li>
<li>Freedom or Order</li>
<li>Honor or Pragmatism</li>
</ul>

<p>These influence:</p>

<ul>
<li>Dialogue</li>
<li>Relationships</li>
<li>Available choices</li>
<li>Political alliances</li>
<li>And ultimately, the ending itself</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>Will you remain loyal to Arthur until the very end?</strong><br>
Will you side with Mordred and help build a new kingdom from the ashes?<br>
Or will you be the head that wears a crown?</p>

<hr>

<h2>Explore, Investigate, Fight — and Race Against Time</h2>

<ul>
<li>Dynamic encounters</li>
<li>Branching investigations</li>
<li>Random events</li>
<li>Dangerous roads and wilderness camps</li>
</ul>

<p>The world does not wait for you.</p>

<ul>
<li>Evidence fades</li>
<li>Witnesses disappear</li>
<li>Conflicts escalate</li>
<li>The kingdom falls into chaos</li>
</ul>

<p>Rest is a choice. Delay is a consequence. Every action reshapes the world behind you.</p>

<hr>

<h2>Tactical Turn-Based Combat with Turn Steal Mechanics</h2>

<p>Classic turn-based battles meet aggressive turn-steal mechanics inspired by real-time action systems.</p>

<p>Different actions consume different amounts of turns:</p>

<p>A longsword slash, a shield guard, and a war hammer strike all move at different rhythms — so do enemies, each with unique combo patterns.</p>

<p>Break enemy rhythm with:</p>

<ul>
<li>Perfect Guard</li>
<li>Guard Break</li>
<li>Interrupt</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>Fight carelessly, you die in seconds.</strong><br>
<strong>Fight skillfully, you dominate the battlefield.</strong></p>

<hr>

<h2>A Literary Roleplaying Experience Built Around Subtext</h2>

<p>This is not about mastering systems. It is about reading what is not said.</p>

<p>The prose is novelistic, dense, and sensory — focused on atmosphere and emotional tension.<br>
Dialogue is cinematic and brief, often hiding intention beneath silence.</p>

<p>Characters are fully formed in belief and contradiction. The protagonist is intentionally undefined — shaped entirely by your choices.</p>

<p><strong>Your story is shaped by how you interpret people, situations, and unspoken intentions, and by the choices you make.</strong></p>

<hr>
